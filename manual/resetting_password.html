Resetting Password



Download Video


To reset a forgotten password for your account, click on “Forgot?” in the password input box on the Login page. You will be directed to the Forgotten password page where you can enter your email address. If the email address is associated with an existing account, a new temporary password will be emailed to that email address. Use the temporary password to log in and remember to change your password.

If the Forgot? link is not present, then it has been disabled by the administrator of that ICE instance. Contact the administrator for details on how to reset your password.
