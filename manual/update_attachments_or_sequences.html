Updating attachments

Entries have an attachments section where files can be uploaded and attached to an entry. Attached files can also be removed. Write access privileges are required to attach and remove files from an entry.

Updating sequence

Sequences associated with entries are visualized using the embedded Vector Viewer tool. Supported sequence formats are SBOL 1/2, Genbank or Fasta.
